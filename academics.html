Academics

Academic Overview

At VVHSS Thamarakulam, we offer a well-rounded academic program designed to challenge and inspire students. Our curriculum is designed to foster intellectual growth and prepare students for higher education and beyond.

NCC

The National Cadet Corps (NCC) program at our school provides students with opportunities for leadership, discipline, and adventure. The program includes various training activities, camps, and community service projects.

Little Kites

Little Kites is our early childhood education program aimed at nurturing young minds. We focus on creating a stimulating environment where children develop foundational skills through play and structured activities.

Scout & Guides

Our Scout & Guides program encourages students to participate in outdoor activities, develop survival skills, and contribute to community service. The program aims to build character, teamwork, and leadership qualities.
